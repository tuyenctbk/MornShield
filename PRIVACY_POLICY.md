# Privacy Policy for MornShield

Last updated: July 06, 2026

MornShield ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile, Wear OS, and Android TV applications.

## 1. Data Collection & Zero-Cloud Policy
MornShield is designed as a **privacy-first** application. 
- **Notification Data**: When the "Morning Shield" is active, notifications are processed in volatile memory (RAM) only. No message content, sender information, or notification body is ever written to persistent storage or transmitted to any external server.
- **Sleep Data**: Sleep tracking data from Wear OS is stored locally on your device's internal database (`mornshield_db`).
- **Ritual Logs**: Your morning ritual completion logs are stored locally.

## 2. Health Connect Integration
If you choose to sync with Android Health Connect, MornShield will only write ritual completion events to the local Health Connect store. We do not upload this data to our own servers.

## 3. Local Network Sync
MornShield uses Network Service Discovery (NSD) and local TCP sockets to sync data between your Phone, Watch, and TV. This communication happens strictly over your local Wi-Fi network and never leaves your home environment.

## 4. Ads
We use Google AdMob to serve ads in the free version. These ads may collect device identifiers for personalized advertising. Premium users have all ad tracking disabled.

## 5. Your Consent
By using MornShield, you consent to our local-only data processing model.
