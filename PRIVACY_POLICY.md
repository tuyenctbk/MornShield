# Privacy Policy for MornShield

Last updated: July 22, 2026

MornShield ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile, Wear OS, and Android TV applications.

## 1. Data Collection & Zero-Cloud Policy
MornShield is designed as a **privacy-first** application with a strict **zero-cloud** policy:
- **Notification Data**: When the "Morning Shield" is active, notifications are processed in volatile memory (RAM) only. No message content, sender information, or notification body is ever written to persistent storage or transmitted to any external server.
- **Sleep Data**: Sleep tracking data from Wear OS (calculated locally using device sensors like accelerometer and heart rate) is stored locally on your device's internal database (`mornshield_db`).
- **Ritual Logs**: Your morning ritual completion logs are stored locally on your device.

## 2. Local Network Sync
MornShield uses Network Service Discovery (NSD) and local TCP sockets to sync data between your Phone, Watch, and TV. This communication happens strictly over your local Wi-Fi network and never leaves your local home network.

## 3. Ads
We use Google AdMob to serve ads in the free version. These ads may collect device identifiers and standard advertising metrics for personalized and non-personalized advertising. Premium users have all ad tracking disabled.

## 4. Data Retention Policy
Because MornShield is a zero-cloud application, **we do not store, retain, or process any of your personal user data on our own external servers.** 
- All sleep data, ritual logs, and app settings are stored locally and exclusively on your physical device.
- This local data is retained on your device for as long as the application remains installed, or until you choose to delete it.

## 5. Data Deletion Process
Since we do not host or store your data on external servers, there is no personal data stored in the cloud for us to delete. 
- You can delete all locally stored data and settings at any time by clearing the application's storage or cache through your Android device settings:
  1. Open your device's **Settings**.
  2. Navigate to **Apps** -> **MornShield**.
  3. Select **Storage & cache** and tap **Clear storage**.
- Alternatively, uninstalling the MornShield application will automatically delete all local databases and associated user data from your device.
- For any questions, support requests, or assistance with deleting data, you can contact us at: **tuyenctbk@gmail.com**.

## 6. Your Consent
By using MornShield, you consent to our local-only data processing and storage model.
